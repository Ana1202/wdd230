Targer Audience

The two personas that as a chamber of commerce would target could be divided in to two. The first persona is: Business owner and entrepreneurs, as a chamber of commerce we would strive to get membership of as many business as possible. The second persona would aim to target the community, for they are who truly move the economy and the business around town.

PERSONA ONE

The first persona: Edgar Banuelos Age: 50 years old Occupation: Local Business Owner

Edgar is the owner of a local landscaping design company. He has served the community for over 35 years; his best marketing strategy has been word of mouth and returning customers, he does not even have a website. He does work on hardscaping and landscaping also does a wonderful job with water features and fountains. However, he knows Evanstown is growing and he is looking to grow and modernize his business. For a while now he knows he has missed jobs on new constructions sites because new people in town do not know about him. While doing his research, he wants to get help from someone local. Edgar just found out about Evanstown Chamber of Commerce having classes and resources available with other local business. He is discovering the great benefits of joining the towns chamber of commerce by helping local business owners grow, get modernized and finding new customers for many years to come.

PERSONA TWO

Second persona: Marian Smithson Age: 35 years old Occupation: Girls Softball Coach at Bous and Girls club

Marian, has been working at a local Bous and Girls Club as an athletic coach for the girlsâ€™ softball team for almost 5 years. She knows the difference the club makes on kids, specially to the lower income part of the community of Evanstown. However, every year the number of kids coming to the B&G club has increased but the equipment and uniforms for the little league has not. She is worried because of low donations the budget cuts will result in less spots available for kids to join. She has been thinking about looking for local business to help sponsor equipment and maybe uniforms but is not exactly sure how to do or who to approach. On her search Marian has learned the Evanstown Chamber of Commerce does fundraisers through local business to help the community on various programs. Also, the Chamber of Commerce connects local business that want to help the community and possibly sponsor local activities to help Evanstown growth and Equality, so everyone has the opportunities to succeed. Marian will find great resouces and connection because of the Evanstown Chamber of Commerce.

Scenarios:

1.- Target Demographic: New Entrepreneur. Starting a business needs help with permits and regulations with the city. Will use this website to look up classes open for new and inspiring business owners, resources and more.

2.- Target Demographic: Local Farmers. Looking to supply fresh products to local restaurants. Will be able to use the chamber of commerce website to connect and view list of local restaurants.

3.- Target Demographic: Local Business. Wanting to get more involved in community, will be able to use web site to find community events, sponsor opportunities, service events and connection to many of local organization to connect people with business and business with community.

4.- Target Demographic: Tourism and Vacation. Possible tourist will be able to review the site to find activities, important fun and local places to sight see and annual and seasonal activities available for the town.

5.- Target Demographic: Well, Establish Business and Employment Seekers. We want the community to grow, so the chamber of commerce would be a great resource to those that are looking to hire with those looking for work!
