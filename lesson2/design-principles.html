White space and Clean Design and Constrast

Odd pears

  • https://www.oddpears.com/

    • For this website I chose the principles of white space, and contrast. The reason for that Odd Pears can recognize the bold designs and colors of their product and use white space as contrast to not over crowd their design with other colors, backgrounds, and fonts. For this page white space is a great tool to balance and better organize content to improve the visual communication experience. Also, white space/background is use as contrast to highlight their product and make it stand out.

    Visual Hierachy

    Academy Sports + Outdoor

  • https://www.academy.com/

    • Academy Sports Store has done a good job with visual Hierarchy as the arrangement of elements to show their order of importance. If one pays notice on the picture, they have more than one sale going on, the first one is more eye catching, which is a the top of the screen with color background and bold dramatic font. Then, the second sale which, is where the hierarchy comes into play, not only is placed lower on the screen. The font while still in color is less dramatic, more even in sized with a white background.
